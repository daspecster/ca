Car Advocates

How it works!

  1. Send us as many details about your future car as you can!
  2. We'll give you some information about the car or class of car you're searching for and possibly ask a few more questions.
  3. Once you've pointed us in the right direction, we'll search our approved dealerships and contacts to find your car!
  4. Lastly, we encourage you to rate your dealer and car to help other car lovers out there!

Why?

We love to see people love their cars! We found that our experience at car dealerships wasn't unique to our friends. It's rare to find a car dealership that has salesman that really know a lot about the cars they're selling. It's also rare to find salesmen that want to get you in a car you'll love even if it's not on their lot at that moment.

Our goal is to put you in a car that you'll fall in love with and connect you with dealerships that genuinely want to help you.
